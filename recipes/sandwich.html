Ham and Cheese Grilled Sandwich

The best way to improve on a great grilled cheese is to sprinkle some cheese on the outside of the bread. It creates a super-crisp, cheesy crust.

Ingredient List:

  • 4 tablespoon of butter
  • 8 slices of bread
  • 1/2 cup of Parmigano Cheese
  • 1/2 pound of sliced cheese
  • 8 ouncles of thinly sliced ham
  • Pickle slices
  • 2 table spoon of Dijon Mustard
  • 1/4 Cup of Apricot Reserves

Directions:

  1. Butter each slices and prinkle with Parmigano
  2. Invert the slices onto a work surface with cheese side down
  3. Top 4 of the slices with Swiss Cheese, ham and pickles
  4. Mix the mustard and spread the remaining 4 slices of bread
  5. Grill sandwiches over moderate heat until golden crisp on the outside
